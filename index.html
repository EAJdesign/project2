CONTROLLED CHAOS

CC

I move about through my weeks, changing hats constantly each day, and by evening I find myself wondering, how did I get through that, and more importantly, how will I do it again tomorrow? The places I go to and from, the people I interact with, the spaces I visit, and the things I do there, are all layers that create my chatoic life. Somehow, although chaotic, it feels controlled. In my mind there is a structure of groups by place, and within each place, the people, spaces and things associated are encompassed. These days happen in a similar style all week long, with smaller occurances that define them, or current projects to tell them apart, but above all they are very much the same. I view my daily routine as a controlled chaos that may never end, but only morph into the life I am working towards (which actually spurred the chaos in the first place).
